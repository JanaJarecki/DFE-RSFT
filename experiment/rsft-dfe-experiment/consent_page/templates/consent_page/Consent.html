{% blocktrans trimmed %} Dear participant,

thank you for your interest in this study. The following information helps you to decide if you want to take part in the study.

This study's aim is to investigate human decision making. The study will take approximately {{duration}} minutes to complete. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} During this study you will be asked to make decisions on the computer. Further, you will be asked to answer socio-demographic questions, for instance about your gender and age. The decisions include attention-check decisions, which are easy decisions with an obvious best answer. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Risks{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Participation in this study poses no known risk to your health. The questionnaires can contain questions of personal nature, for instance about your financial situation. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Remuneration{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} This study is financed by the Center for Economic Psychology at the University of Basel. You will receive a compensation of {{ participation_fee }} for the invested time and attention. Additionally, you can win a bonus payment. The exact height of the bonus depends on your decisions during this study.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Data Use, Storage, and Publication{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} All data collected in this study will be stored and analyzed anonymized – referring to data without information that could identify individuals according to the current state of technology. The data, which is collected in this study, will be used for scientific research. The findings of this study will be summarized and presented at scientific conference, in a scientific journal, and other medias. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} The anonymized data – referring to the data without information that could identify individuals according to the current state of technology – will be made publically available in an online database to be analyzed by other researchers, wich serves to accelerate scientific progress. Before publicizing the data, we will delete or alter all information that could, according to the current state of knowledge, lead to an identification of your person. Especially any data concerning your IP-address, name, date of birth and e-mail address will be deleted. The data made public will include information such as your age, income, educational degree, or occupation. Importantly, by participating in this study you are agreeing to the publication of your anonymized data. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Withdrawal{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Your participation is entirely voluntary. You can, at any time, end your participation during the study without stating any reason. You can withdraw your consent to the data collection and storage at any time during the study and without stating any reason. Please note that after the end of this study, you can withdraw consent to usage of your data, but, because of the anonymized data storage, we will not be able to remove your data from the dataset anymore at this point in time. {% endblocktrans %}
